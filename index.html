Biography

I am currently working as a Visiting Research Fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems in Saarbrucken, Germany. Since the completion of my undergraduate degree in May 2017, I have been involved in active research at IBM Research India and IIT Delhi before joining my current position in Germany. The core of my research in the undergraduate years and since has been along the lines of computational linguistics, artificial intelligence and social network analysis.
