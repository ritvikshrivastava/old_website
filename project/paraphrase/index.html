Paraphrase Detection

 

During my internship at IIT Delhi, I was thoroughly involved in the fields of Natural Language Processing and Social Network Analysis. The first problem that I studied under the guidance of my mentors was that of Paraphrase and Semantic Similarity Detection.

The engineered system involved a machine learning based model using novel hand-crafted features. SVM(Support Vector Machine) was used as the preferred ML tool.
